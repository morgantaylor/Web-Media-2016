KFOG

Objective

Build an entirely new looking site from a Wordpress template. Keeping in mind things that we weren't able to change (display ads/trackers, and main promotion carousel). The reason for the shift in appearance was the radio station was looking to rebrand and wanted something fresh and modern to go with the change. Doing something completely new in attempts to shake up what people inside and outside the industry think of when they think of a radio station website.

Challenge

Change the entire structure of the website without having access to changing the actual HTML of the site. I was only able to touch the CSS and add in some minimal JavaScript.

Solution

I really wanted to bring the same information in a better way. Photos and graphics are attract people much more than static words. We put a high emphasis and prioity on graphics and photos to attract people to the new style and layout. People are accustumed to cards and what kind of informatin they contain. Adding white space and having a real purpose for every element is something most radio station website don't do.

Learned

I wasn't entirely sure how i was going to do this rebuild initially. Before this project I had never dug into how to restyle the structure of a Wordpress site. I went one piece at a time and did a lot of testing within the developer tools pf Chorme's browser to see what would happen. My entire rebuild was based on trial and error.
