Bay Area Food Banks - KGORadio

Objective

The whole point was to bring awareness to people about the Bay Area Food Banks and how people can help donate.

Challenge

Displaying several different food banks and giving information about them so people could contact them or find them to donate in a clean easy way.

Solution

I used a google map with customized icons for each of the Food Banks in the Bay Area and matched them up with the larger icons above the map so people could match them up. One you click on one of them in the map you get all the informatoin you need. Whether that is directions from where you are, their website, or their phone number.

Learned

I really embraced and learned how much white space and balance information.
