Bay Area Food Banks - KGORadio

Objective

We had a client that wanted to use our station platform and presence to bring awareness to visitors about the Bay Area Food Banks and how people can help donate.

Challenge

My challenge was finding a way to display the food banks in a fun, colorful, and inviting way. This would draw visitors to seek more information about the food banks; for example, contacting them or donating to them, which was the ultimate goal. By taking a minimalistic approach people could get the information they needed without being overwhelmed by information.

Solution

We used custom icons for each of the food banks and matched them up with icons on a Google map. Once you click on one of the map icons all the information about that food bank will pop up generated by Google. Therefore keeping the actual page from being filled with unnecessary information to the user. The information displayed could be directions from where you are to the food bank, their website, or their phone number.

Learned

I really embraced and learned how much white space can enhance a page and how to keep it balanced.
