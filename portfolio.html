Design Process

Discovery

I start by interviewing the client and asking questions to understand where solutions can be implemented.

Research

Researching the industry and competition is very important. After all assets are collected i compile the site architecture ensuring the flow is intuitive for users.

Design

Through iterations of sketches, mockups, and prototypes, all visual elements and individual page designs are created and presented for feedback.

Development

After the mockups have been approved i get started on coding each individual website to pixel perfection. It is important to me to do extensive browser testing as well as testing for the best optimization.

Launch

Once getting the clients final feedback the site can go live.
