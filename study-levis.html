KFOG Levi's® Lounge

Objective

Create an online space to house all the online content that comes out of the Levi's® Lounge performance space.

Challenge

My main challenges for this project were finding a way to integrate it into KFOG.com, find a way to navigate once on the page, organizing the different types of content on the page in a way that people would intuitively understand, and being consistent in the way content is presented.

Solution

We split the header area of the page to display featured upcoming events/contest and then static text on the right inviting people into the space online and in person to what Levi's® Lounge is. The contest has also been sectioned to three categories (performance, intervews, and photo's). Those categories are represented with a color coding system as well as a name that accompanies the content on the individual card based on what type of content it is. This color coding, for now, illiminates the need for culky navigation. Having another navigation section would take away from the main KFOG navigation and could confuse visitors. The color coding trains the visitors with how the content will continue to be displayed on this page as well as through out the site. It helps with the overall change in KFOG.com.

Learned

I have learned what it's like to work on a project long term with walking through problems, ideas, and solutions with insternal departments and the outside partner. Finding a way to make them happy with the product while still producing clean code that can continue to grow. Knowing that I may not have direct communication but that my main ideas and points come across in the work was important and remains important while this project is still on going on KFOG.com as well as other radio station websites in the cluster.
