KNBR FranFest Insiders Guide Presented by McDonalds

Objective

Find a modern and engaging way to display information that would be usefull to people who are going or are at FanFest. Had to be responsive as the main use would most likely be while at the actual event as it's happening.

Challenge

Finding a way to integrate McDonalds in a way they liked and making them feel tied into the event.

Solution

Cards are not only a trendy form of digesting content they also represent great user flow. People are now trained enough to understand what a card is and what it represents.

Learned

I honestly learned how much responsive design plays into user happiness. That's something I was already aware of but I got to watch first hand while people on Facebook and Twitter shared this page with friends. Seeing the pace at which it was shared and viewed, most likely at the event on mobile devices, really made responsive design much more real to me.
