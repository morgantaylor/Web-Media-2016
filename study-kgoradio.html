KGO Radio

Objective

Both KGORadio.com and KFOG.com were going through complete structural transformations. KGORadio.com shifted their focus from being a predominantly News station to Talk Radio. That meant a huge shift of the content to be displayed on the website. The objective was two-fold; I needed to find a way to make sure the website didn't seem empty without all the normal news content. Secondly, I needed to rebrand it as a Talk station rather a news one.

Challenge

The challenge with the rebranding was potentially losing an audience that expected news content. We needed to find a new niche for the radio station itself, while finding a new digital identity and presence.

Solution

The majority of all the elements stayed the same structurally, however there were some stylistic changes. For example, a lot of the hard edges were replaced with rounded corners and stark colors were replaced with toned-down colors. My ultimate goal was to make sure the site was cohesive. Consistency was and continues to be key through all the stationâ€™s websites.

Learned

What works the best on this site is how pliable the cards on the homepage are. They adapt at each stage of our posting process: between putting up the initial guest page before a show then after the show updating that same post with a podcast. As a viewer it seems like things are constantly changing but in reality, on the backend, only small adjustments are being made with minimal additions being added.
