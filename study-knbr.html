Auction Pages

KNBR/BASHOF Sports Auction & KNBR/Griants Community Fund Auction

Objective

Display between 10-25 different packages for the auctions.

Challenge

Finding a way for people to view those packages in a clean way that isn't cluttered and also easily viewed on mobile devices. I wanted people to be able to view one package and its details at a time and have those details dissapear when you click on a different one.

Solution

Using CSS transforms and transition i was able to simulate what Javascript could have also done. When you click on a package an accordion drops to reveal the details of the package. When you click on another package it opens and the previous one closes.

Learned

Not being able to use Javascript inside of Wordpress was a challenge but after researching how to use CSS3 to its full potential i was able to simulate transitions with pure CSS which is really the only thing Wordpress won't strip out.
