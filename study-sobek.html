Mountain Travel Sobek - KFOG

Objective

Create a client microsite on KFOG.com where listeners could enter to win a trip sponsored by Mountain Travel Sobek.

Challenge

Let images speak for themselves and showcase the trips that people were able to win. Gather submissions with PHP and database setup where people could choose which trip they wanted to win from three choices. Needed to be responsive.

Solution

For these sites that live inside the radio station websites structure is key. Keeping a block layout made it a responsive first layout. The images are the first thing you see when coming to the page and before you even know its a contest you've picked which one you want as your trip. All you need to do is put in your name, email, and which trip you want. Then you're entered.

Learned

Working with databases, PHP, and responsive design isn't as easy inside a Wordpress oriented website. Fixing the small details and bugs paid off in the end to have a cohesive presence for the client.
